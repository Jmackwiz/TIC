About

Wisconsin-based photographer capturing the world through a unique lens. My work explores the intersection of nature, street photography, and experimental techniques.

With my Nikon D7200, I seek to reveal the extraordinary in the ordinary, finding beauty in unexpected places and moments that often go unnoticed.

My photography is an extension of my philosophy: to explore, to question, and to see the world with fresh eyes every day.
